# Buttery Privacy Policy

Effective July 3, 2026

Buttery stores an account identifier, email address, username, display name, sign-in provider, and
optional profile picture when an account is created. Recipes, albums, favorites, grocery lists,
and cooking progress are stored on the user's device. Recipe content and selected media are
uploaded when a user intentionally shares a recipe.

Buttery uses this information to authenticate accounts, display profiles, separate users' content,
and deliver shared recipes. Buttery does not sell personal information and does not use advertising
or cross-app tracking.

Buttery uses Google Firebase for authentication, profiles, recipe sharing, database services, and
uploaded media. Google Sign-In and Sign in with Apple are available when selected by the user.
Those providers process information under their respective privacy policies.

Users can edit their display name and profile picture, delete their content, or use Delete My
Account in Settings to remove their account and associated Buttery cloud data. Guest content
remains only on the device.

Account information is retained until the account is deleted. Shared data remains until an
involved account deletes it. Buttery uses authenticated Firebase access controls, but no system can
guarantee absolute security.

Buttery is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal
information.

This policy may be updated as Buttery changes. The effective date will be revised when material
changes are made.
